IT-Related Ethics

In the field of Information Technology, ethical behavior is crucial. Developers must ensure they respect users' privacy, create fair systems, and act responsibly when handling data.

The ACM Code of Ethics emphasizes that computing professionals should contribute to society and human well-being, avoid harm, and be honest and trustworthy.

Reference: ACM Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct (2018).
